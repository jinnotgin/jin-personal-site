## Why it existed

Timezone conversion is simple in theory, but annoying in practice.

## The friction it answered

Most timezone tools made users deal with timezone mechanics instead of asking in ordinary language.

## What was built

A chatbot that processed natural-language queries and returned timezone conversions without making users handle the underlying complexity.

## What it left behind

An early NLP-flavoured personal project, archived as part of the older maker history.
